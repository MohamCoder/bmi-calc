Health Tips

How to Calculate BMI

You can calculate your BMI using an online calculator or by following this formula:

BMI = weight in kilograms / height in meters squared


What is the normal BMI range?

The normal BMI range for adults is 18.5 to 24.9. A BMI of 25 to 29.9 is considered overweight, and a BMI of 30 or higher is considered obese.


What are the health risks of being overweight or obese?

Overweight and obesity are associated with an increased risk of many chronic diseases, including heart disease, stroke, type 2 diabetes, and some types of cancer.


What can I do to lose weight or maintain a healthy weight?

There are many things you can do to lose weight or maintain a healthy weight, including:

  • Eating a healthy diet
  • Being physically active
  • Making small changes to your lifestyle, such as drinking less sugary drinks and eating more fruits and vegetables
